License

We are still waiting for final confirmation of the license for the data from UKRLP.

The data on consortia and linkset to dbpedia are CC0, you may use them for any purpose (but a credit is still nice, if possible).

Provenance

The core data of University IDs, names, homepages and addresses are supplied periodically from UK Register of Learning Providers.

The mapping to consortia and dbpedia identifiers is maintained by the learning-providers.data.ac.uk webmaster (currently Christopher Gutteridge.
