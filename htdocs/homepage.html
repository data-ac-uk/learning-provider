This site provides open linked data about UK Learning Providers. Initially the only data available is for Universities but this map be expanded in time.

We use learning-provider rather than "institution" or "university" as the codes we are using to uniquely identify universities are "UK Learning Provider Reference Numbers" (UKPRN). We only (currently) resolve URIs for universities, but we may expand the service to include data on all learning providers in the future, but no promises.

RDF Data

All these are in the "Turtle" RDF format.

Learning Providers (augmented) - 252K
(If you want ALL the data from this site, this is the document). RDF Descriptions of all the learning providers data supplied from http://www.ukrlp.co.uk/, and augmented by additional (non-authoritative) data created by the webmaster of this site; such as wikipedia page and what groups and consorita they are members of.
Learning Providers (basic) - 180K
RDF Descriptions of university data supplied from http://www.ukrlp.co.uk/. It should be considered as being from an authoritative source.
University Groups and Consortia - 26K
RDF Descriptions of groups and consortia (eg. Russell Group), including name and website of members.
Linkset to "Gateway to Research" URIs - 22K
RDF Turtle containing just a list of owl:sameAs startments connection the URIs for learning providers to the Gateway to Research URI for that organsation, where available.
Linkset to DBPedia - 22K
RDF Turtle containing just a list of owl:sameAs startments connection the URIs for learning providers and consortia to the DBPedia URI for them.
DBPedia Data for UK Institutions and Consortia - 18185 Triples - 3.2Mb
RDF Turtle containing the DBPedia triples for the consortia and institutions. This could be downloaded direct from DBPedia but this document combining the 177 relevant documents is provided for convenience.

The data on consortium/groups and linkset to DBPedia and Gateway to Research are CC0, you may use them for any purpose (but a credit is still nice, if possible).

The mapping from UKPRN to HESA IDs is taken from the HESA Website, but no formal license is specified.

Corrections

The core data of University IDs, names, homepages and addresses are supplied periodically from UK Register of Learning Providers.

The mappingconsortium/group and dbpedia identifiers is maintained by the learning-providers.data.ac.uk webmaster (currently Christopher Gutteridge).

The postcode geo locations are from http://data.ordnancesurvey.co.uk/.

If the data here is incorrect or out of date, please Let us know.
